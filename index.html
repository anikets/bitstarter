Some mockups of the proposed app.

Travlster will help public commuters with:

  • Finding information about buses, local trains and other modes of public commute.
  • Users will be able to visit nearby bus stops and railway stations and get information, right from the comforts of the app. Saves time and effort.
  • Know exactly where you have to get off the bus. No anxiety of missing your destination stop.

As a Travlster supporter, you will get access to all features of the app for an entire year.
